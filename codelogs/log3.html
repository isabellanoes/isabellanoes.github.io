What is CSS Media Query?

These work to assign specific qualities to sites based on the user's mode of viewing; ex. phone, tablet, PC screen, or laptop. If someone has a screen that reaches 600px, you can rewrite the code to match a 14px font size. These sites are then called "responsive" designs. Media queries are useful because they ensure the quality of your design is always at its best.
