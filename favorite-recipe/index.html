Penne Alla Vodka

Penne pasta so creamy, it will transport you to a better place.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces Penne Rigate
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup vodka
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup basil

Directions

  1. Bring pot to boil
  2. Add pasta, take out when al dente
  3. Meanwhile, heat deep skillet on medium, adding olive oil and onions
  4. Cook onion for five minutes or until softened, and then add garlic
  5. Add vodka into skillet mix and cook until evaporated
  6. Add tomato sauce and salt and bring to a simmer
  7. Pour heavy cream and stir until simmer
  8. Remove pan from heat and add parmesan, stirring until melted
  9. Toss the pasta and basil into the sauce
