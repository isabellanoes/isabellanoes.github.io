Penne Alla Vodka

Penne pasta so creamy, it will transport you to a better place.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces Penne Rigate
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup vodka
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup basil
